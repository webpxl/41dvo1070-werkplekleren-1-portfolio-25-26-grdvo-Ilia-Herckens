About me

I am a Belgium-based creative with a strong focus on minimalism, clarity, and thoughtful design. My work is rooted in calm, balance, and timeless aesthetics, while occasionally introducing bold or aggressive elements to break that calm in a controlled and intentional way. I believe in contrast as a design tool — using tension to create impact without losing refinement.
